236Hot New MoveThe worker recently invented a new move or spot that everybody is talking about; they get a bonus to their performance in every match. [This attribute is automatically temporary and lasts between three months and two years.]
237Hot New CatchphraseThe worker recently invented a new catchphrase that has caught on in a big way; they get a bonus to their performance in every angle in which they get to talk and a boost to their merchandise sales. [This attribute is automatically temporary and lasts between three months and two years.]
238Flavour Of The MonthThe worker recently did something that has everybody talking about them; they get a small bonus to the rating of every match and angle they are in to simulate the increased interest, plus a boost to their merchandise sales. [This attribute is automatically temporary and lasts between six months and two years.]
241Midas TouchThe worker is enjoying a sudden burst of amazing form; they get a bonus to their performance in every match. [This attribute is automatically temporary and lasts between three months and two years.]
